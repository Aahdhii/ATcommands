AT+CSQ – Signal Quality
Reports the Received Signal Strength Indicator (RSSI) and Bit Error Rate (BER).

Response format: +CSQ: <rssi>,<ber>

AT+CPIN? – SIM Status Check
Checks whether the SIM card is ready, needs a PIN, or is missing.

Response format: +CPIN: READY or +CPIN: SIM PIN

AT+COPS? – Network Operator Info
Returns the current mobile network operator info.

Response format: +COPS: <mode>,<format>,<operator>
